Summary

My project was about learning to become a professional hacker, but that is not the kind of job that you can learn to do over the course of a school year. So, I instead focused on building skills that would serve me well once I eventually do gain all of the required experience and education to hack as a job. Throughout the Course of this project, I gained a lot of knowledge and skills, including:

  • Basic Linux command line skills
  • Javascript
  • HTML & CSS
  • Networking )both the computer and the people kind(
  • Nmap
  • Virtualization with VirtualBox and Xen
  • Security Best Practices
  • Burp Suite
  • And many other skills

While all of these things will likely be extremely useful in my hacking career, there are some things that are hard to get from teaching yourself. This is why I am taking an Information Technology course at Mountainland Technical College. This will likely be an important step toward my eventual goals of becoming a professional hacker.
